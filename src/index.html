Thank you for all of your feedback! Please read below how the updated version functions.

Click or tap the medals below to mark them.

  • Click or tap once to mark the challenge "bought" (Black & white, thick grey border)
  • Click or tap it again to mark it "in progress" (Faded colors, thin red border)
  • Click or tap it a third time to set it to "conquered" (Fully colored, no border)
  • Finally click or tap it again to unmark the challenge (Black & white, no border)

You can show or hide the discontinued medals that you have not conquered (and are not bought or in progress) by checking the checkbox below.

When you are done, click on the "generate image" button to create a single image of your collection that you can save.
